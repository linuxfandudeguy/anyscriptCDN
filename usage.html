How to Use AnyScriptCDN

Base URL: https://anyscript-cdn.vercel.app/

  • NPM: /npm/:packageName/@:version/:filename
  • Yarn: /yarn/:packageName/@:version/:filename
  • PNPM: /pnpm/:packageName/@:version/:filename

Examples

  • /yarn/react/@latest/umd/react.production.min.js
  • /npm/lodash/@4.17.21/lodash.min.js
